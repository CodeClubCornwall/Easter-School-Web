> Want to be a Coding Super Hero?

Are you aged 15 to 18 and interest in a career in software development? Join us for a 4 day experience at Redruth, working in a software development team on an exciting project.

Whatever your level of experience our team of mentors are there to support and help you, giving you a taste of what a career in Software Development is like.

If you are already a Star Coder, that's great and we will use your skills and help prepare you for your first software development job.

If you have never written any software before, don't worry, that's great too. We'll show you how it's done and get you started on your journey to becoming a Coding Super Hero

> The Project Mission to Mars

Meet our Robots, Bert and Ernie. Your task will be to write the software that will allow this robots to safely explore a remote environment.

Using a combination of basic electronics, Raspberry Pis and Arduinos, you will learn and use Agile tools and techniques to great some great software that will enable the robots to autonomously explore a hostile environment.

> The Experience
What will I learn?

You will be part of a team of 4, working alongside other teams with the aim of building a working autonomous robot. You will learn about:

  • SCRUM
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Linux
  • Arduino
  • Programming in C
  • Basic Electronics
  • Team Working
  • Test Driven Development (TDD)

> FAQ

  • Where is the training taking place?

    The teams will be based at Bluefruit Software in their new offices at Industrial Estate.

  • How much does it cost?

    Nothing! The experience is being funded through a European Social Fund Project, Cornwall College and members of Software Cornwall.

  • What will do?

    We are creating a realistic working environment so you can experience what it is like to work in the software industry. You will be expected to act as though you are at work, working hours will be 9am until 4.30pb.

  • I've don't know how to code, can I still come?

    Yes! If you are motivated and want to explore software development as a possible career, then this is for you. We have a team of mentors that will help you to learn coding whilst being part of a self-organising successful team.

  • Will I get paid?

    Unfortunately not, this will be a fun learning experience that may help you with getting a job or applying for further courses.
